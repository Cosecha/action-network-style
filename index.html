3 Day Email Course on Cosecha

Start: April 18, 2016 • 11:00 AM • Arizona (GMT-07:00)

Disclaimer: this is not going to the same as the Cosecha National Assembly. The only way to get that experience of building a movement for mass mobilizations and training is to register and show up.

So what is this Three Day Training over email supposed to be? Easy:

  1. Primer or refresher on the strategy and structure behind Cosecha
  2. An easy to follow format. Check the email when you have time. Go through the material at your own pace. Forward the emails you think your friends need or will resonate with.

So sign up now!

On the morning (or afternoon for Midwest & East Coast folks) April 18, you will get the first email in your inbox. Consider that your first training session. The next day, you will get another. And the finally, on the third day the last session will be in your inbox.

Again, take it at your own pace. It can be a lot of material to take in. Only you know the pace your are comfortable with. You don't have to finish the material in the 3 days.
